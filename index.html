📏 The Lineup Rules

Hard rules that govern every lineup decision.

RULE #1 Evan + Teddy — always together. Chemistry, leadership, scoring. Never split.
RULE #2 At least 1 scorer (Evan, Teddy, or Jesse) on the floor at ALL times. Without one, you won't score. This is your hardest rule.
ALWAYS Jordan plays with the weaker players. He's the Tier 2 anchor. When Akiva, Eli, or Max are on the floor, Jordan is there to steady them.
ALWAYS Evan attacks LEFT. Lefty advantage. Drill it.
AVOID Never play Max + Eli + Akiva on the floor together. You'll get steamrolled. Spread them across units.
AVOID Never let Max or Eli be the primary option. They're not primary anything. Make sure a Tier 1 or Tier 2 player is initiating every possession they're on the floor for.
MAX RULE Max sets a ball screen, then rolls or clears. That's his offense. No cuts into traffic. No dribbles. Screen, roll, rebound.
MAX RULE On defense, Max guards their weakest player. Or zone up when he's in. Don't ask him to chase their best scorer.
BIG 3 Big 3 (Evan, Teddy, Jesse) — at least 2 on floor together whenever possible. They're your only real scorers. When all 3 rest, offense dies.
